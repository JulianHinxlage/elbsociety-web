Child Safety Policy

Zero Tolerance: We strictly prohibit any content or behavior that exploits, endangers, or harms children. Violations result in immediate permanent account suspension and reporting to authorities.

Prohibited Content & Behavior

  • Any sexualized content involving minors (under 18)
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming or inappropriate contact with minors
  • Soliciting personal information from children
  • Content that facilitates child exploitation

Our Response

When child exploitation is identified, Elbsociety will execute:

  • Immediate content removal
  • Permanent account suspension
  • Report to law enforcement
  • Full cooperation with investigations

Reporting & Moderation

  • Use in-app reporting for suspected violations
  • All reports are reviewed manually by staff
  • Reports are treated with the absolute highest priority

Emergency Reporting

Immediate Danger: Contact local emergency services immediately.
